Church Events

Grace of the Lord Church - Upcoming Events

Welcome to Grace of the Lord Church, a place of worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth. Join us for our upcoming events and be blessed as we seek to grow together in faith, love, and service to our community. All are warmly invited!

20
Fri

Fasting Prayer

Location: Grace of the Lord Church, Kamuli, Uganda

Come and experience a powerful time of fasting and prayer as we seek God’s divine presence and intervention in every area of our lives. Let the Holy Spirit move mightily and bring transformation to your spirit. All are encouraged to attend this special gathering!

21
Sat

Prayer for Sweden and Israel

Location: Grace of the Lord Church, Kamuli, Uganda

Join us in intercession as we pray for God’s blessings over Sweden and the peace of Israel. Let us stand in unity, lifting these nations before the Lord and believing for a breakthrough. Together, we can make a difference through the power of prayer.

22
Sun

Sunday Worship Service

Location: Grace of the Lord Church, Kamuli, Uganda

Celebrate the goodness of the Lord with us in an uplifting Sunday worship service! Enjoy a time of vibrant praise, heartfelt worship, and a life-changing message from God’s Word. Bring your family and friends to encounter the joy and hope of Christ.

23
Mon

Interactive Bible Study

Location: Grace of the Lord Church, Kamuli, Uganda

Discover the treasures of God’s Word in an engaging and interactive Bible study session. Let the scriptures come alive and provide guidance, encouragement, and inspiration for your daily life. Don’t miss this opportunity to grow in your spiritual journey.

24
Tue

Youth Fellowship Night

Location: Grace of the Lord Church, Kamuli, Uganda

Youth, come together for an unforgettable night of fun, faith, and fellowship! Enjoy music, games, and meaningful discussions that will strengthen your faith and build lasting friendships. Let’s grow in Christ and inspire each other to make a difference!

25
Wed

Community Outreach Program

Location: Grace of the Lord Church, Kamuli, Uganda

Be the hands and feet of Christ as we reach out to our community with love and kindness. Join us in acts of service and charity, making a positive impact and sharing the hope of the Gospel. Together, we can transform lives!
