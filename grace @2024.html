Grace of the Lord Ministries

Grace of the Lord Ministries is biblically based on a non-denominational Church where our only aim is to take the Word of God undiluted to the people of nations the world over.

The Lord has commanded us to be a light to the gentiles and salvation to the ends of the Earth. Our burden is to make disciples of all the nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all things Jesus commanded us.
